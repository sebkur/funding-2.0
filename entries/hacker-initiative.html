    Hacker Initiative

    "The Hacker Initiative “HI” is a duly qualified Title 26 US Code IRS 501(c)(3) tax exempt public non profit charity and duly registered under California Corporate Code Title 1 Division 2 and registered with the California State Attorney General Registry of Charitable Trusts. HI’s Mission is to support and grow the collective knowledge and contributions of the worldwide hacker community. We support hackers of all types, regardless of experience — whether that’s the passionate beginner or the lifelong hacker wishing to make a broader contribution to the hacker community and the world."
